How It Works

Complex Legal Procedures Simplified

Step 1 – Choose Your Service

Visit our Pricing page to select the Power of Attorney package that best fits your needs. Our support team is here to help you find the right fit.

Step 2 – Review & Approve Your POA

After collecting your details, we’ll draft a customized Power of Attorney document and email it to you for review. You can make any adjustments before approving the final version.

Types of Power of Attorney We Offer

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long does the entire process take?

Most POA orders are completed within 24 hours. For more complex services, such as document attestation, timelines may vary.

2. What types of Power of Attorney can I obtain?

We offer General, Special, and Medical Power of Attorney options, as well as Property, Business, Vehicle, and Court Representation POAs, among others.

3. Is it safe to pay online with Poas.ae?

Yes, we use advanced security measures to protect your information throughout the payment and document handling process.

4. Do I need to visit an office for the notarization?

No, with our e-notarization service, you can complete the process through a secure video session from the comfort of your home.

5. What documents do I need to prepare?

The required documents depend on the type of POA you need. Typically, you’ll need identification and relevant supporting documents. Our team will provide a detailed list during the process.
